# FMCG Sales Variance Analysis Dashboard

## Description

This project is a Power BI sales variance and forecasting dashboard developed for AlphaNext, a Nigerian FMCG company. The dashboard analyzes sales performance against targets across four sales territories in 2024. It provides insights into salesperson performance, monthly sales trends, variance analysis, and future sales forecasting to support strategic business decisions.

The analysis revealed that the organization exceeded its annual sales target by ₦46.6M (+1.72%), while also uncovering hidden volatility, regional disparities, and seasonal performance fluctuations that impact long-term revenue stability. The solution combines descriptive analytics, variance analysis, trend monitoring, and forecasting to help leadership optimize planning, improve sales execution, and drive data-informed business decisions.

# Business Introduction
AlphaNext operates in the Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) sector in Nigeria. The company manages multiple regional sales representatives responsible for achieving monthly and yearly revenue targets across different territories.

---

# Business Problem
Leadership lacked a centralized reporting system to:

- Compare actual sales against targets in real time
- Monitor salesperson performance across territories
- Identify underperforming periods early
- Understand seasonal sales fluctuations
- Improve forecasting and operational planning
- Support strategic sales decision-making
---

# Aim of the Project
The goal of this project was to:

- Compare actual sales against targets
- Analyze monthly and yearly sales variance
- Identify top-performing and underperforming salespersons
- Detect seasonal sales patterns
- Forecast future sales performance
- Provide actionable recommendations for business growth

---

# Process
The following steps were carried out during the analysis:

1. Data cleaning and transformation using Power Query
2. Data modeling and relationship creation in Power BI
3. DAX calculations for KPIs and variance analysis
4. Creation of interactive dashboards
5. Forecast trend analysis
6. Territory and salesperson performance analysis
7. Business insight generation and recommendation development

---

# Key Insights

## Overall Performance

- Total annual sales reached **₦2.619B**
- Annual target was **₦2.573B**
- The company exceeded target by **₦46.6M (+1.72%)**

---

## Salesperson Performance

### Top Performer: Chioma

- Actual Sales: ₦708.72M
- Variance: +₦36.77M
- Variance %: +5.47%

Chioma exceeded the highest assigned target, demonstrating strong execution capability and high regional sales potential.

### Lowest Performer: Danladi

- Variance: -₦3.35M
- Variance %: -0.37%

Danladi underperformed relative to assigned targets, suggesting possible regional, operational, or performance-related constraints.

---

## Monthly Performance Trends

### Best Performing Month

- December recorded the strongest performance
- Variance %: +41.45%

### Weakest Performing Month

- February showed the lowest performance
- Variance %: -31.49%

---

## Seasonal Analysis

The analysis revealed:

- Weak performance in Q1 and parts of Q3
- Strong sales spikes in March, October, and December
- Bi-monthly fluctuations across most sales regions

This indicates strong seasonality effects influencing revenue performance.

---

## Territory Intelligence

The project incorporated regional economic analysis by comparing:

- Population size
- GDP per capita
- Territory performance
- Regional purchasing power

### Key Observation

Lagos significantly outperformed other territories due to:

- Higher population density
- Stronger purchasing power
- Higher economic productivity

---

# Business Recommendations

## 1. Introduce Dynamic Forecasting

Replace static annual targets with rolling quarterly forecasts to improve planning flexibility and forecasting accuracy.

---

## 2. Implement Seasonality-Based Planning

Align:

- Marketing campaigns
- Promotions
- Inventory planning
- Sales initiatives

with historically strong sales periods.

---

## 3. Replicate Top Performer Strategies

Document and share Chioma’s sales approach across the organization through:

- Mentorship
- Internal workshops
- Performance clinics

---

## 4. Strengthen Q1 Sales Strategy

Develop targeted campaigns to address recurring underperformance during:

- January
- February
- April

---

## 5. Improve Territory Optimization

Allocate resources based on:

- Regional economic potential
- Historical sales trends
- Market maturity
- Customer purchasing behavior
